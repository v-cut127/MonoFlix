Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I buy tickets?

A: Select a movie and showtime, choose seats, and complete checkout with payment details. You will receive a confirmation email with your booking ID.

Q: Can I change or cancel my order?

A: Cancellation and change policies vary by cinema and event. If change is allowed, use your booking page or contact support with your booking ID.

Q: What if my tickets don't appear after payment?

A: Check spam folders. If not delivered, contact support with proof of payment and booking details.

Q: Do you share my data with advertisers?
